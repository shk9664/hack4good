The Problem: Food Waste The Solution: Urban Grocery

The Issue: Food Waste

- 1.3 billion tons of food wasted worldwide

- 3.3 billion tons of carbon dioxide produced to make wasted food

- 40% of food in Canada is wasted

- 47% at home

- 183 kg of food wasted per person per year 

The Cost

- $1 trillion worldwide; $31 billion in Canada

- $28 per week per household

- 1/4 of wasted food could feed 795 million undernourished people

- 850,000 Canadians use food banks each month

Our Objective.

Reducing our footprint together as a community.
